About Your AI Coach

"Everyone deserves a personal coach. Not just the people who can afford $150/month. Everyone."

The Problem

Personal training is one of the most effective ways to get fit. A good trainer keeps you accountable, designs programs for your body, plans your meals, and pushes you when motivation drops. But personal trainers cost $50-150 per session. That means the people who need coaching most — beginners who don't know what to do, people who struggle with consistency — can't access it.

Meanwhile, "AI fitness apps" are just chatbots with workout templates. They generate a list of exercises and call it a day. No accountability. No voice coaching. No meal plans. No understanding of your psychology or behavior patterns. When you skip a workout, nobody notices. When you want to quit at week 3, nobody's there.

The Solution

Your AI Coach was built to solve this. It's not a fitness app — it's a personal coaching relationship delivered through software.

The AI coach literally calls your phone before your gym sessions and at the end of each day. It generates workout programs specifically tailored to your body scan and biometrics. It creates meal plans with high-protein, easy-to-make, delicious recipes from your regional cuisine. It scans your food with your camera for instant nutrition analysis. It tracks your body composition over time. And behind every interaction, 13 intelligence modules work to understand your behavior, catch avoidance patterns, support you through dark moments, and adapt how it communicates to match your personality.

All for almost free.

The Technology

Your AI Coach is a full-stack product built from the ground up:

  • Voice Calls — Real VoIP calls via professional voice infrastructure with native phone integration
  • Coach Personality — 44,000-token personality engine with emotional intelligence, linguistic mirroring, and 5 intensity tiers
  • 13 Intelligence Modules — Anti-skip detection, dark moment protocol, behavioral profiling, communication DNA, milestone detection, pattern detection, memory management, personality engine, micro challenges, social proof, ED safety detection, autonomous coach actions, and conversation style variation
  • Progressive Overload Engine — Epley 1RM tracking, plateau detection, and intelligent plan triage
  • Region-Aware Nutrition — Cuisine-specific meal generation for 13+ regional food cultures
  • Computer Vision — Gemini AI-powered food scanning and body composition tracking
  • Safety Systems — Eating disorder detection, calorie floors, and toxic language prevention

The Mission

To make world-class fitness coaching accessible to everyone, everywhere, regardless of their budget. We believe AI can deliver a coaching experience that rivals — and in many ways surpasses — what a human coach can provide, at a fraction of the cost.

Your AI Coach is available on iOS and Android.
