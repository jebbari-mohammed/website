Why is Protein Important?

Protein is the building block of muscle. When you work out, you create micro-tears in your muscle fibers. Protein provides the essential amino acids needed to repair those tears, making your muscles bigger and stronger.

How to Hit Your Target

Hitting a high protein target can be challenging. We recommend spreading your intake across 3-4 meals. Focus on lean meats (chicken breast, turkey, lean beef), fish, eggs, Greek yogurt, and supplement with whey protein if needed to reach your daily goal.
